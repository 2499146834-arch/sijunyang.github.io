03 — Projects

Selected work

Individual Project 02/2026 – 05/2026
FinBridge — Knowledge Distillation for Financial LLMs
Problem
Open-source 7B LLMs hallucinate badly on financial QA — unusable for audit-sensitive deployments requiring traceable reasoning.
Data
FinQA benchmark plus teacher traces distilled from a commercial LLM, covering numeric reasoning and multi-step financial calculations.
Approach
LoRA fine-tuning of Qwen2.5-7B on LLaMA-Factory. Student was forced to emit executable "computation programs" instead of direct answers; ran 6 controlled ablations to quantify each training stage.
My Contribution
Sole owner — data pipeline, LoRA configs, ablation design, evaluation methodology, and final reporting.
Outcome / Impact
FinQA accuracy improved 22% → 65.39% (+43.4 pts), with auditable step-by-step outputs and single-GPU deployment on an RTX 5060 Ti. Hallucinations sharply reduced versus base model.
Individual Project 02/2026 – 03/2026
Cats vs. Dogs — Two-Stage Transfer Learning for Mobile Deploy
Problem
An original from-scratch CNN reached only 89% with errors concentrated in low-light scenes — blocking mobile deployment.
Data
Cats vs. Dogs benchmark (25K images) augmented with a targeted low-light augmentation pipeline.
Approach
Rearchitected to MobileNetV2 with a "freeze-backbone then fine-tune" two-stage strategy. Led the production redesign end-to-end.
My Contribution
Led the rearchitecture for production — full ownership of model selection, augmentation design, evaluation, and deployment readiness.
Outcome / Impact
Accuracy rose to 98.98% while model size shrank ~9× — far better suited for mobile deployment than the original CNN.
Individual Project 02/2026 – 03/2026
Real-Time Parking Detection — Dual-YOLO Fine-Tuning
Problem
Smart-parking scenes mix fixed CCTV with handheld phone shots, which no single detector handled well in real time.
Data
Two heterogeneous splits — fixed CCTV footage plus handheld phone imagery — kept at native resolution for true-scale fine-tuning.
Approach
Designed a "specialist + generalist" dual-YOLO setup with true-scale fine-tuning in the final stage. Diagnosed and fixed recurring VRAM crashes during training.
My Contribution
Sole owner — dataset split design, dual-model training, VRAM debugging, and benchmark comparison against the original paper.
Outcome / Impact
Both models exceeded 99.4% accuracy and 99.7% precision / recall — a 5.4-point gain over the original paper, meeting industrial real-time requirements.
Group Project · Team "Latent Space" 01/2026 – 02/2026
AODL — CIFAR-100 Image Classification (ResNet-18 + SE Attention)
Problem
Reference baseline reached only 72% Top-1 with architectural claims (the SE attention "gain") unvalidated by controlled experiments.
Data
CIFAR-100 benchmark — 60,000 colour images (32×32 px), 100 classes, 50K training / 10K test split. Trained on CUDA GPU with PyTorch.
Approach
Rebuilt the pipeline in PyTorch with cosine annealing, label smoothing (0.1), and mixed-precision training. Ran 3-seed controls isolating the SE attention module.
My Contribution
Co-led experiment design, owned the ablation pipeline and multi-seed reproducibility study — surfaced that the SE claim did not hold up. Contributed to the final report and presentation.
Outcome / Impact
Top-1 accuracy rose to 75.66% with 1.5–2× faster training. Controlled study showed the SE block gave no significant gain on this task — correcting the original claim.
